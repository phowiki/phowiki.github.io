The Florida Gatorman

The Gatorman appeared in late 2008 and has mostly been operating out of Pittsburgh. His first crime was stealing two alligators out of a habitat in the Atlanta zoo. A staunch defender of animal rights, he is known for jailbreaking animals from zoos. It appears he does not work with any groups and is often seen carrying around the two alligators with him on his crime sprees.

Powers

Speculated Tinker 4, Brute 2, Mover 2, Blaster 2, Master 4. It is surmised that The Florida Gatorman's specialty is Tinker tech revolving around animals. This is noted by the design of his power suits, often incorporating characteristics of animals that he has stolen. His power suit consists of leg units with the ability to jump over literal buildings, a jetpack that appears to be manufactured after a hummingbird, arms based on gorillas, and clamps that function as alligator jaws. He has also been seen shooting sprays of venom.

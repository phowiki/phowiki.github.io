Bumble

Bumble is seen mostly in a support capacity for the Hardcore Four. It is surmised that he does not like to enter directly into combat as he appears incredibly clumsy and accident prone.

Contents

  1. Powers
  2. References

Powers

Shaker 4. Bumble appears to have the ability to inflict of field of unluckiness. The degree of the effect is random. It appears he can change who it affects, or at least what it affects, but in this area things that can go wrong are more likely to do so. While knowledge of his power is abstract, what we do know about it has come from reports of Thinkers that have interacted with him in confrontation.

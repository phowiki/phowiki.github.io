Threat Levels

D Class: Anyone possessing superhuman capabilities that would be a threat to the general population.

C Class: Anyone possessing superhuman capabilities that would require a cape or an entire PRT unit to contain.

B Class: Anyone possessing superhuman capabilities that would require multiple capes and Multiple PRT units, as well as specialized loadouts to contain.

A Class: Any threat that has the possibility to duplicate or exponentially grow, and would require a large concerted effort from the PRT to contain.

S Class: Rated amongst the most dangerous threats in the world. Any power that creates more instances of power generation or recurring effect in an epidemic pattern. Could also be a Tinker with the ability to create a true AI or any threat with global implications. The main difference between an S and A level threat is that the Triumvirate and key members of the Protectorate are guaranteed to intervene. Known S level threats: Behemoth, Leviathan, The Simurgh, The Slaughterhouse 9, Nilbog, and Sleeper.

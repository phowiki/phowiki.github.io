Booster

Seen operating solo the most often, Booster is an associate or possible member of the Hardcore Four. Booster's closest tie appears to be with C.O.G., and can often be found causing mayhem and other acts of vandalism with him. Booster on several occassions has been seen riding a car as if it were a surf board, presumably to steal it. Other times he is seen pulling "stunts" such as jumping canyons on a skateboard, hardcore parkour, and BASE jumping with no parachute.

Powers

Striker 4, Mover 3, Brute 2. Booster appears to have the ability to manipulate kinetic energy through touch. He can use this to launch objects or himself. In combat he uses this to throw projectiles at great speed, close distances between himself and enemies, and literally launch combatants out of the area. It appears he also has slight Brute endurance as he has been seen surviving landings exceeding 50 miles per hour.

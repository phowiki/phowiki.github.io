Edgelord

Edgelord is the apparent leader of the Hardcore Four. His appearance consists of a large trench coat, typically with a graphic tee underneath and a biker helmet in the shape of a skull. Apparent age is anywhere between 16 and mid-twenties. Crimes include petty theft, assault and battery, obstruction of justice, possession with intent to sell, jaywalking, grand theft auto, and reckless driving, etc.

Powers

Shaker 3, Mover 1. Edgelord's power consists of the ability to extend the edge of any surface with a force field. This can be used both offensively and defensively, as he can extend the edges of blades for an attack or the edges of walls to block incoming attacks. He has also been known to create surfaces to drive on to further his escape and create surfaces to walk on for the same purpose. The upper limit of his extension is unknown, as is the rate at which he can extend. Force fields do, however, seem to disappear after he leaves the proximity.

C.O.G. (Center Of Gravity)

C.O.G. appears to be the least active member in the criminal activies of the Hardcore Four, usually taking a passive role in combat, taking to mocking combatants rather than engaging.

Contents

  1. Powers
  2. References

Powers

Shaker 3. As his name suggests, C.O.G. has the ability to alter his and other people and object's center of gravity. This has been used to disorient others, increase his mobility and balance, and to cause detrimental effects on architecture. This ability has been used to dismantle bridges in order to aid their escapes.
